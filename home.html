  • Lasagne

  • Butternut Pumpkin

    Butternut pumpkin is a cream-colored winter squash with sweet orange flesh. Rich in vitamin A and fiber, this pumpkin is ideal for soups, roasts, or salads, offering a delicious and nutritious taste.

  • Tokusen Wagyu

    New $39.00

    Tokusen Wagyu is a high-quality beef from Japan, known for its perfect marbling (intramuscular fat). The meat is tender, juicy, and rich in flavor, offering an exquisite culinary experience.

  • Panna Cotta

    $25.00

    Panna Cotta is an Italian dessert made from cream, sugar and gelatin. The texture is smooth and creamy, often served with berry or caramel sauce. It offers a light and refreshing sweetness.

Our Strength

  • Hygienic Food

    Fresh environment is one of the main hallmarks of our restaurant, creating a pleasant and refreshing atmosphere for every diner. We believe that the dining experience is not only about the food, but also about the surrounding environment.
